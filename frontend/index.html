About The Fest

What is AVLOKAN?

A platform where engineering students, faculty, and industry professionals collaborate, compete, and innovate over three electrifying days.

AVLOKAN is the annual technical festival of the Association of Civil Engineers (ACE), Department of Civil Engineering, Birsa Institute of Technology (B.I.T.) Sindri.

Conducted over three days, AVLOKAN brings together engineering students from across the country to apply classroom theory to real-world problems — with emphasis on civil infrastructure, sustainable development, and digital engineering.

The festival features a 12-hour inter-college Hackathon, Technical Paper Presentations, AutoCAD Drafting Contests, Civil Engineering Quiz, Survey Treasure Hunt, and concludes with cultural performances and a grand valedictory ceremony.

Special Event

Survey Treasure Hunt

A technical navigation event — surveying puzzles, field tasks, and intellectual decoding across the campus.

01

Team Formation

5 teams of up to 5 members each, randomly mixed from 1st & 2nd year students. Separate male/female groups. Each team picks a unique one-word name.

02

Device Policy

Only team leader carries a mobile phone. Google Maps, ChatGPT, and passwords for decoding are permitted. Live location sharing via WhatsApp is mandatory.

03

Rules of Conduct

No communication between teams. Full participation required. Losing materials = elimination. Misbehaviour = immediate disqualification.

04

Boundaries

Commences at the Gate of Civil Engineering Department and concludes at the Department Garden.

Challenge Progression

  • 1 Draw "Cheat Sheet 1" to determine first direction + receive secret Bonus Card for the final level
  • 2 Pass oral MCQ tests and technical dictation in Civil Engineering at checkpoints
  • 3 Solve coordinate-based puzzles (Latitude & Longitude) to find next locations
  • 4 Perform verbal password exchanges with officials to verify identity
  • 5 Complete field work: top-view sketches, collecting environmental materials
  • 6 Decode ASCII texts to unlock a password-protected PDF and find the treasure
  • 7 Final Validation: Present all items (nail marks, journal thread, gitti, chalk) + password exchange
