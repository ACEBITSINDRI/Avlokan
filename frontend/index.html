About The Fest

What is AVLOKAN?

A platform where engineering students, faculty, and industry professionals collaborate, compete, and innovate over three electrifying days.

AVLOKAN is the annual technical festival of the Association of Civil Engineers (ACE), Department of Civil Engineering, Birsa Institute of Technology (B.I.T.) Sindri — organised to bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world civil engineering challenges.

Conducted over three days, AVLOKAN brings together engineering students from across the country to apply classroom theory to real-world problems — with emphasis on civil infrastructure, sustainable development, and digital engineering.

The festival features a 12-hour inter-college Hackathon, Technical Paper Presentations, AutoCAD Drafting Contests, Civil Engineering Quiz, Survey Treasure Hunt, and concludes with cultural performances and a grand valedictory ceremony.

Previous Editions

AVLOKAN has been fostering innovation and technical excellence at B.I.T. Sindri for years.

AVLOKAN'25

The inaugural edition featured a 12-hour hackathon, technical paper presentations, AutoCAD contests, and a civil engineering quiz. Students from multiple colleges participated, showcasing real-world engineering solutions.

2024

RISCE-2024 International Conference

Bringing together researchers, academicians, field practitioners, and policy makers at a common platform to share their experiences and knowledge in the field of research and innovation to solve real-life problems in a cost-effective manner for the well-being of the Global Community.

2024

ACE Technical Meet

Before AVLOKAN was branded, ACE organised departmental technical meets with intra-college quiz competitions, mini-project exhibitions, and guest lectures from civil engineering professionals.

Project Exhibition Guest Lectures Quiz Competition
2023

Workshop on Remote Sensing & GIS

The department organised a hands-on workshop on Remote Sensing and Geographic Information System (GIS) applications in Civil Engineering, training students on modern surveying techniques, satellite imagery analysis, and spatial data processing for infrastructure planning.

Remote Sensing GIS Training Spatial Analysis Hands-on Workshop
2023

Departmental Fest

The Civil Engineering department hosted seminars, workshop on survey instruments, and a campus-wide treasure hunt — laying the foundation for what would become AVLOKAN.

Survey Workshop Seminars Treasure Hunt
